Welcome to SND

Sultan Naga Dimaporo (SND) is a vibrant municipality in Lanao del Norte that beautifully blends cultural heritage with modern development. Our town offers unique experiences where tradition meets progress.

From pristine beaches to historical landmarks, SND is a hidden gem waiting to be discovered.

About SND

Our Rich History

Sultan Naga Dimaporo, formerly known as Karomatan, is a 1st class municipality in the province of Lanao del Norte, Philippines. With a population of over 50,000 people, our town is a vibrant community that celebrates its cultural roots while embracing modernity.

The municipality was renamed in honor of Sultan Dimaporo, a respected leader in the region's history. Today, we continue to honor our traditions while building a progressive future for all residents.

Geography and Climate

Located in the northern part of Lanao del Norte, Sultan Naga Dimaporo enjoys a tropical climate with beautiful coastal areas and fertile inland regions perfect for agriculture.

Our town boasts diverse ecosystems from mangrove forests to coral reefs, supporting rich marine biodiversity.
